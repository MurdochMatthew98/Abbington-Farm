Abbington Farm Menu

Find out more info about our delicious breakfast, brunch and lunch menus!

Breakfast!

Choose from our delicious breakfast menu.

Our farm shop has a wide range of fresh produce and products, from jams and preserves to eggs and bacon.

We have free-range eggs that are laid by the chickens on our farm.

Enjoy some of our award winning breakfast meals such as: two large free range eggs on toast; free range ham and eggs; waffles served with maple syrup and mixed berries or banana; or a classic fry-up!

As well as that we have fresh sandwiches; a selction of porridge, healthy cereals or yoghurts for a healthy start OR choose from our selection of cakes.

We also have freshly brewed coffee, sweet teas and soft drinks.

Or you can customize your very own breakfast meal.

We also have vegetarion and vegan options!

Brunch, Lunch + Afternoon Tea!

Pop in and enjoy our brunch menu!

We have a wide variety of fresh homemade pies filled with blackberry; apple; strawberry; etc.

Or get something from our lunch menu. Popular favourites include: bangers and mash; ham, egg and chips; ham hock hash; or our classic cheeseburger.

There are vegetarian and vegan options; as well as fresh salads.

You can also enjoy one of our classic smoothies; freshly brewed coffee or select from our range of sweet teas!
