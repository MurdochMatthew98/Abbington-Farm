Activities

Check out some of the fun activities we have available!

Corn Maze

Head out to the field and have fun navigating the twists and turns in our corn maze!

Visit our small pumpkin patch to find and pick your favourite pumpkins!

Hayride

Explore the farm on a guided tour in one of our hayrides!

Be a farmer for a day and use our farm tools, such as a pitch fork or shovel!

Visit our Garden

Pick your own produce from the garden to take home with you!

Visit The Animals

Visit our animals and see how they live in their natural habitat!
