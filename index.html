About Us!

We are a small, family-owned farm turned visitor attraction situated between Edinburgh and Carlisle.

Our farm shop has a wide range of fresh produce and products, from jams and preserves to eggs and bacon.

We have free-range eggs that are laid by the chickens on our farm.

Our pork is reared in the fields around our house, giving it a distinctive flavour.

Choose from award winning pies with fillings such as: apricot or blackberry; hand-made chocolates; local honey and marmalade; homemade breads and cakes; locally sourced with fruit juices, wines, beers and ciders.
