Local Walks and Play Park

Find out some more about the walks available in the local area or our free to play in play parks!

Walks

Take advantage of our local hiking spots and explore the nearby forest.

With various hiking routes situated around the farm, it is a perfect chance to explore the local scenery and come back to the farm for a bite to eat.

There is a suitable route for everyone, passionate hikers, casual explorers, kids and even pets!

Play Park

Our play park is a great park to spend a few hours in for the kids.

We have various slides, climbing obstacles and other features for the kids to enjoy.

Plus, there's even a small pond with swans and ducks!
